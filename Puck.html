Puck (2021)

for toy piano ~1'

Program Note

Puck (2021) is a miniature for toy piano which was inspired by a specific technique. It is a study on the instrument’s capacity to be percussive rather than melodic. I chose the title Puck, because I felt that the piece was evocative of both a fairy and a demon.
