Magenta Variations (2018)

for clarinet ~9'

Program Note

This work was commissioned and premiered by Amanda Forest. It was written in the fall of 2018 and was premiered in the spring of the following year. It's named after the pitch class G which she has always associated with the color magenta.

For my performer, friend, and muse Amanda.
