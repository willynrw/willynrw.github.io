Good Boy (2023)

for 2-channel audio ~1'

Program Note

Good Boy (2023) is a short work for fixed media which shares material with my work Pastorale for viola and electronics. It consists of sounds which humans make to play with animals, specifically dogs, and how those sounds can be grouped based on their sonic content. There isn't a dog in this piece, however.
