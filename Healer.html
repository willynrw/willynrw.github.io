Healer (2024)

for alto saxophone and 2-channel fixed media ~3'

Written for Nathan Robinson

PROGRAM NOTE

Healer is a piece on the theme of healing which draws inspiration from the role of a healer in role-playing games. I was interested in the differences between in-game healing and healing in the real world. Both have their own iconographies and associated sounds.
Healer was written for the fourth edition of Timbre, an online workshop organized by New Music Mosaic and was premiered in an online format.
