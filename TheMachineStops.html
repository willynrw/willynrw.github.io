The Machine Stops (2021)

for flute, piano and video-conferencing platform ~10'

pre-recorded or live

Program Note

The Machine Stops, for flute and piano (optionally socially distanced) is inspired by E. M. Forster’s science fiction story of the same title (1909). Forster’s dystopian vision is one of a global engineering failure which many people have likened to the Covid-19 pandemic. This work was written for an opportunity specific to the pandemic and was written shortly after the Texas power crisis of 2021. During the ice storms I also discovered Forster's story. This piece expresses the anxieties I had with both crises while I was an international student in Texas, and the pleasure I had reading The Machine Stops for the first time.
