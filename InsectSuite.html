Insect Suite (2017)

for piano ~8'

Program Note

Insect Suite is a catalogue of ideas which I had improvised at the piano over a period of years. It is also a series of humorous miniatures that are relentlessly fixated on their extra-musical subject matter.

I – Butterfly
II – Swatting Flies
III – Grasshopper
IV – Inch Worm
V – Click Beetle
VI – Cicadas
