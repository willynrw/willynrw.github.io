Program Note

White Sky Over the Lake is a generative piece for soloist and electronics. Although the instrumentation is variable it is limited to mallet percussion and keyboard instruments to complement the electronic accompaniment. The performer reads a score which is generated in real time along-side an accompaniment that is also freely generated.

Imagine a nearly still body of water and an empty sky. It is not obvious to the eyes where they meet, or even if they are separate things.
