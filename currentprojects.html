Current Projects

Healer for Saxophone and Fixed Media

From now until November I will be working with saxophonist Nathan Robinson on a short piece about healing and high fantasy.
I am exploring the sonic language of video-games and the aesthetics of role playing games. My working concept is that the listener is experiencing what it is like to be magically healed by the performer.
This project is part of the peer-led proffesional development workshop Timbre organized by New Music Mosaic.
