Gott for organ

I have started to go through Charles Sandford Terry's collection of the J.S. Bach's four part chorales. And I would like to make a piece which catalogs every chord that Bach wrote to accompany the word "Gott" in these pieces. I am not sure whether this will be a monumental piece which I mount at a later date, or short-form content to release on my social media.
Of the first 405 chorales more than half of them contain the word at least once. Some chorales have as many as seven different chords set to the word.
Below is a recording of me playing through chords from the first 18 chorales. I give at least 15" to each chord and break up each 'chorale' with silences of about 5".

Sound of the Anthropocene

My miniature Good Boy(2023) for fixed media will likely be presented at an online gallery based in Cologne, Germany. From what information I have, it will be presented alongside the works of more than fifty other sound artists, from around the world. This will be an ongoing presentation from July 13th into mid August.
