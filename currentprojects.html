Gott for organ

I have started to go through Charles Sandford Terry's collection of J.S. Bach's four-part chorales. I am going through each chorale and cataloging every chord that Bach wrote to accompany the word "Gott." I am hoping that a simple musical presentation of these chords can function as a conceptual piece.
Below is a recording of me playing through chords from the first 18 chorales. I give at least 15" to each chord and break up each 'chorale' with silences of about 5". Playing through the first 405 chorales in this way would take almost 3 hours.
