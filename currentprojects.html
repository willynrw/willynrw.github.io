Current Projects

Rastrum for soloist and video score

Over the summer I will be working with multi-instrumentalist Gage Salnikowski on a piece for solo improviser and video score.
I am working on a graphic score in Processing and Jitter which will be ready by and projected alongside Gage.
We have received partial funding for this project from the Manitoba Arts Council.

Upcoming Performances/Releases

ATMI National Convention

Robin Meiksins will be performing CHIRP (2023) for piccolo and electronics. This piece was premiered by Robin last year at the SPLICE institute at Western Michigan University.
This year's convention will be held at the Washington Hilton in early November.

Vox Novus

This organization regularly holds concerts of miniatures titled 15-minutes-of-fame. I have one 15-m-o-f premiere upcoming, my work Ground (2024) for accordion will be played by Stas Venglevski.
Also David Bohn may be releasing recordings of taishōgoto pieces, which didn't quite make the cut for a 15-m-o-f last year, among them my miniature Volta (2023).
Date TBD.

Sound of the Anthropocene

My piece Good Boy (2023) for fixed media will be presented at an online exhibition based in Cologne, Germany. It will be presented alongside the works of more than one-hundred other sound artists, from around the world.
It will be presented online from August 11 - September 8.
